How it works

A simple flow designed for practical grease management in food establishments.
1

Detect grease buildup

Sensors monitor grease accumulation inside the trap and send readings to the control unit.

2

Activate the transfer process

When grease reaches a defined threshold, the system can activate the pump to move grease to a storage tank.

3

Track storage and maintenance

The dashboard shows storage level, pump status, and alerts so staff know when action is needed.
